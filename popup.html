Tabnet keeps your tabs organised .... always

[Alt+Shift+L] Arrange all tabs by domain Arranges all open tabs by their hostname and auto renames the tab group accordingly.

[Alt+Shift+K] Add tab to a group That special case when you want to create a group by the the title of the tab.
[Alt+Shift+J] Pin unpin current tab Pins the current tab if not pinned. If the current tab is pinned its unpinned.
[Alt+Shift+X] Hibernate the current tab Hibernates a tab and frees up memory. Hibernated tabs are still visible on the tab strip. They are reloaded when activated.
